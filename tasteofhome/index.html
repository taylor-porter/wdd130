Taste of Home

Welcome to Taste of Home! Whether you’re like me, and you’re just learning how to cook, or you want to improve your cooking skills, I hope we can learn together. My name is Taylor Porter, and I am leaving home for the first time as a freshman at BYU-Idaho.

Living away from home has been exciting and challenging, and part of the challenge is knowing what to eat. I definitely miss my parents’ cooking, and want to learn how to make those same home-cooked meals (for myself and the ladies).

It’s been intimidating having to plan meals, go grocery shopping, and cook things I’ve never cooked before, but it has also been an exciting challenge. Learning how to cook has been a fun process, and I’m excited to learn more. Let’s explore the world of cooking together!
